## How to use these videos

1. **First pass - John Savill AZ-400 Study Cram** (one sitting, 1.25x speed, take notes only on what you don't understand).
2. **Second pass - Savill's AZ-400 Master Class** for any domain where you didn't feel confident. Pair with the matching domain-specific card above.
3. **Third pass - Microsoft DevOps Lab + GitHub channel** for product-team-level demos when a feature behaviour question stumps you.
4. **Final pass (D-2)** - re-watch only your bookmarked videos + your own notes section.
5. Then take the **Practice Assessment** (see that page in the sidebar) before scheduling the real exam.

> Tip: AZ-400 questions almost always offer 2-3 *technically correct* options. The right answer is the one Microsoft considers **modern + managed + least-privilege** (e.g. OIDC over client secrets, environments over YAML conditions, workspace-based App Insights over classic). Savill's framing maps 1:1 to that bias.

Pair every video with **hands-on practice** in the [Hands-On Labs](15-hands-on-labs.md) page - passive watching alone won't pass the exam.
